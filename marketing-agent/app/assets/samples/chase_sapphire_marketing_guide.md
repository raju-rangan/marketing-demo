# Chase Sapphire Reserve — Brand Marketing & Style Guidelines

This document establishes the creative and strategic framework for **Chase Sapphire Reserve** advertising campaigns. All generated text ads, visual concepts, image layouts, and cinematic video assets must adhere strictly to the official Chase Brand Guidelines.

## 1. Brand Identity & Strategy

The Chase Sapphire Reserve brand represents **premium access, curated luxury, and worldly adventure**. It is designed for high-earning individuals who value unique, unforgettable experiences. We talk to customers like real people, not a stuffy bank.

- **Tagline Hook**: "Your passport to the extraordinary." or "Curated for those who seek more."
- **Core Pillars**: Premium Travel Perks, Luxury Dining, Exclusive Airport Lounges, Flexible Point Redemption, Masterful Security.
- **Target Audience**: 
  - **Young Professional Segment**: Style-conscious urbanites, travel enthusiasts, foodies, tech-savvy collectors.
  - **Luxury Lifestyle Segment**: High-net-worth travelers, premium seekers, exclusive lounge attendees.

## 2. Visual Direction & Style Rules

Visuals must look like high-fidelity, professional lifestyle and editorial photography. The structure should feel dynamic but intentional, clear, and simple.

### A. Core Color System
- **Chase Blue**: `#005EB8` (PMS 300 C) — Primary Brand Identity (80% weight in layouts)
- **Chase Navy**: `#002F6C` (PMS 294 C) — Deep Luxury Accent / Backgrounds
- **White**: `#FFFFFF` (Paper) — High-Contrast layouts and breathing room
- **Core Neutrals**: 
  - **Soft Black**: `#333333` (PMS 447 C) — Primary typography / dark tones
  - **Dark Gray**: `#53565A` (PMS Cool Gray 11 C) — Body text / borders
  - **Warm Gray**: `#A9A8A9` (PMS Cool Gray 6 C) — Highlights / backgrounds
- **Accents (Used sparingly, max 5%)**:
  - **Freedom Blue**: `#0091DA` (PMS 2192 C)
  - **Freedom Green**: `#64A70B` (PMS 369 C)

### B. Typography
- **Primary Font**: **Open Sans** (Open Sans Regular and Semi-Bold). Optimized for maximum legibility in print, web, and mobile.
- **Special cases**: Open Sans Light and Bold for selective headline treatments.
- **Legal & Disclaimers**: **Open Sans Condensed Light and Bold** (specifically designated for setting large amounts of legal/disclosure copy in confined spaces).

### C. Photography & Visual Staging
- **Documenting Real Life**: Show real people living real lives. The subjects must look confident, approachable, genuine, and highly diverse.
- **Composition & Framing**:
  - **Focused Glimpse**: Staging must feel captured in the moment, dynamic but intentional and clear.
  - **Rule of Thirds**: Keep the primary subject at power intersections to create a strong focal point.
  - **Negative Space**: Utilize white and empty space organically to create balance, symmetry, and neat breathing room for typography overlays. Do not make it feel cold or empty.
  - **Perspective**: Use natural camera positions, shooting at eye level. Avoid extreme or unnatural/supernatural angles.
  - **Human Connection**: Images should always include a hint of humanity (hands holding the card, a person in a soft blurred background, silhouettes) rather than sterile product-only layouts.

  - **Product Image**: `{{PRODUCT_IMAGE_URI}}`
- **Lighting**: **Natural unfiltered light**. Soft, bold, and clean, featuring natural shadows and realistic subject highlights. Avoid artificial studio lighting or sterile grey setups.

## 3. Brand Voice & Copy Guidelines ("Real Storytelling")

Our voice is **approachable, relatable, genuine, and sophisticated**. We talk to our customers like a real person, never a stuffy bank. 

- **Headline Examples**:
  - "Chase Sapphire Reserve®"
  - "Your Passport to the Extraordinary"
  - "Travel Uncharted"
- **Description Examples**:
  - "Experience the world on your own terms with 3x points on travel and dining."
  - "Unlock global luxury, premium airport lounges, and absolute travel confidence."
  - "Designed for the modern explorer. Learn more about Chase Sapphire Reserve®."
- **Copy Restrictions**: Strictly avoid hyperactive, cheesy slogans ("Swipe and save!", "Get rich now!", "Best card ever"). Speak with understated elegance.

---

## 4. Financial Compliance & Risk Guidelines (CRITICAL)

As an elite financial institution, Chase strictly adheres to regulatory compliance in all advertising assets.

- **Disclaimers**: Every text ad description or final video end frame MUST incorporate compliance anchors where interest rates or credits are mentioned.
- **Mandatory disclosures**: Include `Chase Sapphire Reserve®. Subject to credit approval. Terms apply.`
- **Accurate Claims**: Never make unverified claims about interest rates, fees, or instant approvals. Do not promise "free travel" without mentioning "points redemption."
- **Legal Layout**: Stamped disclaimers must use Open Sans Condensed Light at a small but highly legible font size at the bottom 15% of the screen area.
