# Chase Freedom Unlimited — Brand Marketing & Style Guidelines

This document establishes the creative and strategic framework for **Chase Freedom Unlimited** advertising campaigns. All generated text ads, visual concepts, image layouts, and cinematic video assets must adhere strictly to the official Chase Brand Guidelines.

## 1. Brand Identity & Strategy

The Chase Freedom Unlimited brand represents **everyday value, smart spending, and financial empowerment**. It is designed for savvy consumers who want to earn on every purchase without thinking twice. We are the "reliable partner" in their daily life.

- **Tagline Hook**: "Earn on everything." or "Smart rewards for every day."
- **Core Pillars**: Unlimited 1.5% Cash Back, No Annual Fee, Dining & Drugstore Bonuses, Fraud Protection.
- **Target Audience**: 
  - **Everyday Value Seekers**: Families, students, and young professionals who want to maximize their daily spending.
  - **Smart Budgeters**: Practical consumers who value simplicity and reliable rewards.

---

## 2. Visual Direction & Style Rules

Visuals must look bright, energetic, and relatable. The style should feel accessible, warm, and integrated into daily routines.

### A. Core Color System
- **Chase Blue**: `#005EB8` (PMS 300 C) — Primary Brand Identity (60% weight)
- **Freedom Blue**: `#0091DA` (PMS 2192 C) — Energetic Accent (30% weight)
- **Freedom Green**: `#64A70B` (PMS 369 C) — Positive Reward Accent (10% weight)
- **White**: `#FFFFFF` — High-Contrast backgrounds for a clean, open feel.

### B. Typography
- **Primary Font**: **Open Sans** (Open Sans Regular and Semi-Bold).
- **Tone**: Bold and clear for benefit-led headlines.

### C. Photography & Visual Staging
- **Relatable Moments**: Show people in everyday scenarios—grocery shopping, dining out with friends, or commuting. 
- **Official Brand Assets**:
  - **Primary Logo**: `{{LOGO_IMAGE_URI}}`
  - **Product Image**: `{{PRODUCT_IMAGE_URI}}`
- **Lighting**: Bright, natural daylight. High-key lighting that feels optimistic and clear.
- **Composition**: Action-oriented and dynamic. Show the "reward" in action.

---

## 3. Brand Voice & Copy Guidelines ("The Smart Choice")

Our voice is **helpful, optimistic, clear, and encouraging**. 

- **Headline Examples**:
  - "Chase Freedom Unlimited®"
  - "Earn Cash Back on Every Purchase"
  - "The Simple Way to Reward Your Every Day"
- **Copy Restrictions**: Avoid overly exclusive or "luxury" language. Stay grounded in utility and value.

---

## 4. Financial Compliance & Risk Guidelines (CRITICAL)

- **Mandatory disclosures**: Include `Chase Freedom Unlimited®. No annual fee. Subject to credit approval. Terms apply.`
- **Accurate Claims**: Always specify "Cash Back" rather than "Free Money."
