# J.P. Morgan Private Wealth — Brand Marketing & Style Guidelines

This document establishes the creative and strategic framework for **J.P. Morgan Private Wealth** advertising campaigns. All assets must reflect the highest standards of exclusivity, discretion, and financial mastery.

## 1. Brand Identity & Strategy

The J.P. Morgan Private Wealth brand represents **heritage, global expertise, and bespoke financial stewardship**. It is designed for ultra-high-net-worth individuals and business owners who require sophisticated solutions.

- **Tagline Hook**: "Defining the future of your legacy." or "Bespoke wealth management for what matters most."
- **Core Pillars**: Estate Planning, Philanthropy, Alternative Investments, Private Banking, Dedicated Advisors.
- **Target Audience**: Business founders, multi-generational wealth holders, philanthropic leaders.

---

## 2. Visual Direction & Style Rules

Visuals must look sophisticated, understated, and timeless. The aesthetic is "Quiet Luxury."

### A. Core Color System
- **J.P. Morgan Navy**: `#002F6C` (PMS 294 C) — Primary Brand Identity (90% weight)
- **Gold Accent**: `#8A724E` — Used very sparingly for subtle highlights or small logos.
- **Charcoal**: `#333333` — For refined typography.
- **Off-White**: `#F9F9F9` — For a softer, more elegant paper feel.

### B. Typography
- **Primary Font**: **Open Sans** (Regular and Light).
- **Tone**: Understated, minimal, and authoritative.

### C. Photography & Visual Staging
- **Perspective & Depth**: Focus on architectural detail, sweeping landscapes, or quiet, powerful portraiture.

---

## 3. Brand Voice & Copy Guidelines ("The Private Conversation")

Our voice is **authoritative, discreet, visionary, and calm**.

- **Headline Examples**:
  - "J.P. Morgan Private Wealth"
  - "Stewardship for Generations"
  - "The Art of the Long View"
- **Copy Restrictions**: Never use aggressive marketing tactics. Speak with the confidence of a global leader.

---

## 4. Financial Compliance & Risk Guidelines (CRITICAL)

- **Mandatory disclosures**: Include `J.P. Morgan Private Wealth management services are provided by J.P. Morgan Chase & Co. and its affiliates.`
- **Accurate Claims**: Ensure all investment mentions are accompanied by risk disclosures.
