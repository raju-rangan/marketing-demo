# Style: The Contrarian Mythbuster

Use this skill when the video's purpose is to challenge conventional wisdom, break down a common misconception, or spark debate in the comments section.

---

## 1. Video Architecture Blueprint

| Phase | Timecode | Scripting Hook & Formula | Visual Direction | Sound Design |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **The Visual Attack** | `00:00 - 00:03` | *"Most people think [X] is the best way to get [Result], but it's actually costing you..."* | Quick scale zoom on the speaker's face. Heavy, high-contrast text overlay in the center. | **Impact Whoosh** + sharp beat drop. |
| **The Agitation** | `00:03 - 00:15` | Explain *why* the conventional method fails. State the hidden catch. (e.g., *"This standard credit card trick actually devalues your points..."*) | Rapid cut to a split-screen or a clean, bold graphic. 2-second visual cuts. | High-tension, low-bass electronic synth track. |
| **The Climax & Proof** | `00:15 - 00:30` | Reveal the contrarian truth. Provide 1 solid, undeniable data point or visual proof. | Punchy text call-outs. Highlight high-value nouns in neon yellow (`#FFDE17`). | Subdued music; voiceover dominates. |
| **The Infinite Loop** | `00:30 - 00:40` | *"Which is why you should always..."* (Designed to seamlessly flow back to the opening hook sentence). | Abrupt visual cut to black or back to the starting frame. | Music track cuts out instantly on the final syllable. |

---

## 2. Dynamic Typography & Graphics
- **Font Style**: Extra-bold, heavy-weight sans-serif (e.g., Impact or Montserrat Black) centered in the middle 60% of the screen.
- **Color Accentuation**: Limit text to 3 lines max. Color the "Myth" word in Red (`#FF3333`) and the "Solution" word in Vibrant Green (`#39FF14`).
- **Pacing**: Words must pop on-screen in sync with the spoken syllables.
