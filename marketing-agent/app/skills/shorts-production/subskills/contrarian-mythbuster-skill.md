# Style: The Financial Mythbuster

Use this skill when the video's purpose is to challenge conventional financial wisdom, break down a common market misconception, or provide a nuanced, data-backed perspective that counters mainstream retail advice.

---

## 1. Video Architecture Blueprint

| Phase | Timecode | Scripting Hook & Formula | Visual Direction | Sound Design |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **The Thesis Hook** | `00:00 - 00:03` | *"Conventional wisdom says [Common Financial Advice], but the historical data tells a very different story..."* | Clean, medium-close shot of the speaker. A sophisticated, minimal text overlay (e.g., a "Quote" style box). | **Subtle page-turn or soft bass drop** + focused, serious instrumental track. |
| **The Deconstruction** | `00:03 - 00:15` | Explain *why* the conventional method is flawed or overly simplistic. State the hidden variable (e.g., inflation, changing interest rates). | Cut to a high-contrast, professional infographic (e.g., a line chart showing divergence from the myth). | Tempo remains steady. Audio mix prioritizes the clear, authoritative voiceover. |
| **The Data Reveal** | `00:15 - 00:30` | Reveal the contrarian truth. Provide 1-2 undeniable, institutional-grade data points or historical comparisons. | Highlight the key data points on the chart with a smooth, elegant motion (e.g., a glowing line or a soft highlight box). | Subtle "insight" chime. |
| **The Advisory Conclusion** | `00:30 - 00:40` | *"True wealth building requires looking past the headlines. Read our latest market outlook to go deeper."* | Fade to a clean, branded end-screen with a professional call-to-action. | Music resolves smoothly with a corporate, polished tail. |

---

## 2. Professional Typography & Graphics
- **Font Style**: Elegant, highly legible corporate fonts (e.g., Roboto, Open Sans, or the brand's primary serif/sans-serif). Avoid heavy block text or meme-style aesthetics.
- **Color Accentuation**: Use the brand's approved color palette. If highlighting a "Myth" vs "Reality," use subtle, muted brand colors rather than aggressive neon reds and greens.
- **Pacing**: Visuals should dissolve or slide cleanly into place. Avoid aggressive, rapid-fire text popping. The focus must be on the clarity and authority of the message.
