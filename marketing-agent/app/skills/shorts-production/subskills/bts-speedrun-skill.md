# Style: The BTS Speedrun

Use this skill to show complex tech builds, design layouts, or creative assets being constructed from scratch at lightning speed.

---

## 1. Video Architecture Blueprint

| Phase | Timecode | Scripting Hook & Formula | Visual Direction | Sound Design |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **The Climax Hook** | `00:00 - 00:03` | Show the spectacular final product for 0.5s. *"How we built a fully compliant campaign design in exactly 45 seconds..."* | Cut from the beautiful final layout directly back to an empty, raw template screen. | Sharp camera shutter **SFX** + energetic riser. |
| **The Speedrun** | `00:03 - 00:25` | Use rapid, action-oriented verbs. Explain the steps in a hyper-efficient "speedrun" style voiceover. | Sped-up screen recordings (3x-5x speed). Visual jump-cuts every 1.0 second. | High-BPM synthwave or electronic drum & bass track. |
| **The Grand Reveal** | `00:25 - 00:35` | *"And there it is. Fully approved, beautifully styled, and ready to launch."* | Slow-motion zoom into the key visual elements of the finished asset. | Rhythmic music track hits its main "drop." |
| **The Direct CTA** | `00:35 - 00:40` | *"Drop a comment if you want our exact layout file."* | Cut abruptly on a high-energy frame. | Music track halts instantly with a clean reverb tail. |

---

## 2. Speedrun Visual Polish
- **Dynamic Overlays**: Place a tiny timer or progress bar overlay (e.g., `00:15 / 00:45`) at the top right of the screen to emphasize the "speed" aspect.
- **Sound Effects (SFX)**: Every step change or layer click MUST be paired with a subtle "pop" or "keyboard tap" sound effect to maximize psychological satisfaction.
