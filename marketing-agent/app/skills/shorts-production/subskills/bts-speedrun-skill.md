# Style: The Market Speedrun (Data & Process)

Use this skill to show complex financial models, portfolio allocations, or real-time market data being analyzed and constructed from scratch at lightning speed. It builds trust by showcasing technical competence and rigorous methodology.

---

## 1. Video Architecture Blueprint

| Phase | Timecode | Scripting Hook & Formula | Visual Direction | Sound Design |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **The Climax Hook** | `00:00 - 00:03` | Show the spectacular final data visualization or portfolio yield for 0.5s. *"How we analyze 10,000 data points to find emerging market opportunities in under 60 seconds..."* | Cut from the polished final chart directly back to raw data streams or an empty terminal. | Sharp, clean 'data calculation' **SFX** + energetic riser. |
| **The Speedrun** | `00:03 - 00:25` | Use precise, action-oriented financial verbs (e.g., "aggregate," "stress-test," "optimize"). Explain the steps in a hyper-efficient, expert voiceover. | Sped-up screen recordings of charts building, algorithms running, or heatmaps expanding (3x-5x speed). Visual jump-cuts every 1.0 second. | High-BPM, sophisticated electronic or rhythmic instrumental track (think "high-frequency trading" vibe). |
| **The Grand Reveal** | `00:25 - 00:35` | *"And there it is. A fully optimized, risk-adjusted strategy ready for deployment."* | Slow-motion zoom into the key insight or final structured dashboard. | Rhythmic music track hits a satisfying, solid resolution point. |
| **The Direct CTA** | `00:35 - 00:40` | *"Want to see the full analysis? Link in bio to read the report."* | Cut abruptly on a clean, branded end-screen. | Music track halts instantly with a clean reverb tail. |

---

## 2. Speedrun Visual Polish
- **Dynamic Overlays**: Place a subtle, running data counter or progress bar overlay (e.g., `Data Points Analyzed: 8,452`) at the top right of the screen to emphasize the depth of the process.
- **Sound Effects (SFX)**: Every step change or new layer of data MUST be paired with a subtle, premium "tick" or "chime" sound effect to maximize the feeling of precision and technological capability.
