# Financial Marketing & Compliance Skill

## 1. Regulatory Compliance & Risk Management

Advertising financial products (banking, wealth management, lending, credit cards) requires absolute accuracy and adherence to regulatory compliance standards. 

### A. Clear & Truthful Claims (No Deception)
- **Zero Tolerance for Fabricated Claims**: Never promise interest rates, returns, fee exemptions, or point multiplier bonuses that are not explicitly detailed in the campaign brief or product database.
- **No Guarantee of Returns**: When discussing investment opportunities or stock projections, always include clear risk disclosures indicating that performance is not guaranteed.

### B. Mandatory Disclosures & Anchors
- **Pricing Disclosures**: Whenever pricing, rates (APR/APY), fees, or card rewards are advertised, we MUST append compliance text.
- **Standard Anchors**:
  - For Example: `Brand Product Name®. Subject to credit approval. Terms and conditions apply.`
  - Generic Financial: `Subject to eligibility and credit approval. Terms and fees may apply.`
- **Placement**:
  - In Text Ads: Must be included in at least one description or headline where room allows.
  - In Visual Ads: Stamped in clean, legible font at the bottom or in the final end-frame (PMAX/social standard).

## 2. Brand Psychology: Establishing Financial Trust

Financial decisions are driven by a balance of **trust, security, growth, and legacy**. The marketing copy must reflect this.

### A. Tone of Voice Parameters
- **Sophisticated & Informed**: Speak with intelligence and authority. Avoid hyped slang or overly casual sales-pitching.
- **Reassuring & Secure**: Reinforce safety, privacy protection, and financial stability.
- **Aspirational but Grounded**: Showcase real-world luxury, global exploration, or future retirement comfort—without making unrealistic promises.

### B. Emotional Hooks
- **Security Hook**: "Your financial peace of mind, protected by industry-leading security."
- **Access & Privilege**: "Unlock curated global benefits and world-class travel perks."
- **Growth & Future**: "Secure your legacy with tailored wealth management and expert guidance."

## 3. Visual Staging & Color Psychology

Financial aesthetics must convey **exclusivity, elite craftsmanship, and clean professionalism**.

### A. Harmonious Color Systems
- **Sapphire/Deep Blue**: Conveys trust, security, intelligence, and corporate stability.
- **Gold/Bronze Accents**: Represents luxury, prestige, premium quality, and wealth.
- **Platinum/Brushed Silver**: Conveys modern innovation, precision, high-tech capabilities, and high-end membership.
- **Emerald/Forest Green**: Symbolizes growth, financial prosperity, sustainability, and long-term balance.

### B. Visual Staging & Stamped Overlays
- **Real-world environments**: Display products (like credit cards) in context (e.g. placed on a high-end polished wooden dining table, in front of a glowing evening skyline, or in a sleek travel lounge).
- **No Abstract or Sci-Fi Backdrops**: Avoid surreal floating elements, magical particle effects, or cartoonish designs. Visuals must look like actual photos taken by world-class photographers.
- **Disclosures Space**: Maintain a clean 15% padding at the bottom of all visual ads for compliance disclaimers and logo stamps.
